About Me

Hello! I am a Full Stack Developer and freelancer currently studying at Plymouth University. My journey began with a strong foundation in technologies like Java, Node.js, Angular, Python, and React. Recently, a deep curiosity for how interactive worlds are built has led me into the realms of Three.js, Unity, and Blender to create immersive 3D and Interactive experiences. I also enjoy working at the intersection of AI and real world impact, whether it’s building systems, experimenting with LLMs, or turning ideas into something real.

When I'm not deep into code or managing databases and cloud services, you can usually find me exploring UI/UX design trends, learning about game development or adding dynamic animations to my projects. I love blending technical logic with an artistic flair to make the web feel a lot more personal, engaging, and playful.

My ultimate goal is to keep pushing my boundaries as a versatile developer. Whether it's building robust full-stack applications that push the boundary or crafting beautiful virtual rooms, I am always ready for the next challenge.
